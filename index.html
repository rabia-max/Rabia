About Me

Here is entrepreneur, researcher, and lecturer Rabia Iqbal. I am passionate about innovative research tasks due to my background in the field of Biotechnology. I was selected 3 times for college teacher intern in government colleges on a merit base. I worked on “Agricultural Biotechnology Project” and won an entrepreneurship competition by making low-cost fodder systems and animal feed. Presented an innovative idea for complete industrial water treatment in the “District Council Environmental Festival” and won a cash prize. Moreover, I am a comparer and an article writer at many websites like “Biology Decoded”, “Bq Research and Development” and “Petrichores”.
